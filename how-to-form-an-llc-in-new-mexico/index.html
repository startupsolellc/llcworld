1. How long does it take to form an LLC in New Mexico?

LLC formation in New Mexico typically takes 3-4 business days, but expedited options are available for an extra fee.

No, you can use a Registered Agent service like Northwest, which provides a business address.

No, New Mexico does not require annual reports, making it a cost-effective option for business owners.

4. Is there a state franchise tax in New Mexico?

No, New Mexico does not impose a franchise tax on LLCs.

5. Can a non-U.S. resident form an LLC in New Mexico?

Yes, non-U.S. residents can legally form and operate an LLC in New Mexico.

Yes, an EIN (Employer Identification Number) is required to open a business bank account and file taxes.
